Our Process

We follow a proven, collaborative process to deliver high-performing Shopify stores that exceed your expectations.

01. Requirement Discovery & Brand Understanding
We learn about your products, target audience, business model, and brand personality.
  • Understand your products
  • Define target audience
  • Analyze business model
  • Plan store structure
  • Design navigation
  • Define collections
02. Shopify Store Planning
We plan your store structure—navigation, collections, product types, and user flow.
03. Design & Theme Customization
We either custom-design your storefront or modify premium Shopify themes to match your branding.
  • Custom design storefront
  • Modify premium themes
  • Match your branding
  • Build backend
  • Add products
  • Integrate payment gateways
04. Development & Integration
We build out the backend, add products, integrate payment gateways, shipping, taxes, and apps.
05. QA & Mobile Optimization
We test your site on all devices and browsers to ensure speed, usability, and security.
  • Test on all devices
  • Ensure speed & usability
  • Validate security
  • Launch your store
  • Provide training
  • Support post-launch needs
06. Launch & Handoff
We launch your store, provide training if needed, and support your post-launch needs.
