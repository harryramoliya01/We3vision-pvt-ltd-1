Move to Better, Faster, and More Secure Technology—Without Losing What Matters

Is your current tech stack holding you back? We3Vision helps businesses move from outdated systems to modern, high-performing technologies—without data loss, downtime, or disruption. Whether you're upgrading legacy software, shifting to cloud platforms, or changing programming frameworks, we ensure your migration is smooth, secure, and scalable.

From small codebase transitions to full infrastructure shifts, we've got you covered—so your business can grow faster, safer, and smarter.

Our Process

01. System Audit & Migration Plan
We evaluate your existing tech stack, business workflows, and potential risks.
  • We assess your current website—what's working, what's outdated, and where users drop off.
  • We plan the new structure, navigation, content layout, and user journey for better flow.
02. Data Backup & Mapping
We securely back up your systems and map data structure for a seamless transition.
03. Technology Selection
Based on your goals, we recommend the best platforms, frameworks, or architecture.
  • We redesign visuals, typography, and page layouts for a clean and engaging user experience.
  • We rebuild your site on a modern tech stack (or CMS) and migrate content carefully.
04. Migration Execution
We handle backend/frontend migration with zero or minimal disruption to live systems.
05. Testing & QA
We test the new environment for performance, compatibility, and data integrity.
  • We test across browsers, screen sizes, and devices to ensure everything works flawlessly.
  • We optimize meta tags, site speed, and structure to preserve (or boost) your Google rankings.
06. Support & Optimization
We monitor post-migration systems and provide support, updates, and optimization.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I lose my SEO rankings after redesigning?

+
-

No. We follow SEO-safe practices to maintain and improve your rankings.

How long does a website redesign take?

+
-

Typically 2–6 weeks depending on the size and features of your current site.

Can I keep my existing domain and hosting?

+
-

Yes. We can redesign your site while keeping your current domain and host, or recommend better ones.

What CMS or platform will you use?

+
-

We use WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, or custom code—depending on your goals and scale.

Will my site work on phones and tablets?

+
-

Yes. We deliver fully mobile-responsive designs that look and work great on all devices.

What can you migrate—only apps or also servers and databases?

+
-

We can migrate everything—apps, codebases, servers, databases, and even third-party integrations.

How long does a typical migration take?

+
-

It depends on the size and complexity—simple migrations take 1–2 weeks, larger systems may take 4–8 weeks.

Will my existing data and users be safe?

+
-

Yes. We follow strict backup and rollback procedures to ensure 100% data safety and continuity.

Do I need to pause my business operations during migration?

+
-

Not necessarily. We often migrate systems in phases to avoid any downtime.

Can you help after the migration too?

+
-

Absolutely—we offer ongoing support, performance tuning, and security monitoring post-migration.
