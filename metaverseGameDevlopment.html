Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms can you build Metaverse games on?

+
-

We develop for Decentraland, The Sandbox, Spatial, WebXR, and custom Unreal/Unity-based metaverse platforms.

Can I monetize my Metaverse game?

+
-

Yes—we support in-game NFT sales, land ownership, token-based rewards, and smart contract-powered economies.

Do I need to use blockchain?

+
-

Not necessarily. While many Metaverse games include Web3 features, we can also build non-blockchain metaverse experiences depending on your use case.

What makes Metaverse games different from traditional games?

+
-

They're immersive, persistent, social, and often decentralized—blending gaming with commerce, identity, and community.

How long does it take to build a Metaverse game?

+
-

Smaller games or branded worlds may take 4–8 weeks. Full open-world games with Web3 layers typically take 12–20+ weeks.
